About UofK

The University of Kathmandu is one of the most prestigious and renowned universities in the world, located in the heart of the stunningly beautiful Kathmandu valley in Nepal. This university is the epitome of academic excellence , boasting world-class facilities, a highly acclaimed faculty, and an exceptional student body that hails from every corner of the globe. The campus is a sight to behold, with its lush green gardens, ancient temples, and majestic Himalayan mountains towering in the distance. The university also offers a wide range of extracurricular activities, including outdoor adventure sports, cultural events, and community service programs, ensuring that students have a well-rounded and enriching university experience.
